1. A
DISCOURSE
UPON THE
FISHERY.

    _ §. 1. The Fishery is of Great, and Certain Advantage.

    _ §. 2. The Fishery lies fairer for the Subjects of his Majesty of
Great Britain, then for the Hollanders.

    _ §. 3. If the Fishery be Encouraged, and Established, it will prove the
Foundation of an ample, and lasting Revenue to the Crown, and
of Wealth and Prosperity to the Nation.
